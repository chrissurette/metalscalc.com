How to use AI forecasts

Discipline

Forecasts should create a plan, not a promise.

Use scenarios to predefine what you would do if gold breaks higher, if silver fails to confirm, or if macro data reverses the trade.

  • Write down the invalidation level before acting.
  • Compare forecast confidence against market breadth.
  • Do not ignore premiums, taxes, shipping, or spread costs.
