How to use AI forecasts

Discipline

Forecasts should create a plan, not a promise.

Use scenarios to predefine what you would do if gold breaks higher, if silver fails to confirm, or if macro data reverses the trade.

  • Write down the invalidation level before acting.
  • Compare forecast confidence against market breadth.
  • Do not ignore premiums, taxes, shipping, or spread costs.
FAQ

Metals forecasting questions

Can AI predict gold and silver prices?

No model can reliably predict prices. AI and quantitative tools can frame probabilities and scenarios based on history and current data, but precious-metal prices are driven by unpredictable macro and geopolitical events.

How should I use a metals price forecast?

Treat forecasts as scenarios with confidence ranges, not guarantees. Combine them with your own view of real yields, the dollar, and demand, and always confirm live spot price before any transaction.

What factors do metals forecasts consider?

Common inputs include interest rates and real yields, the U.S. dollar, inflation trends, central-bank and ETF flows, industrial demand, and historical volatility and seasonality.
