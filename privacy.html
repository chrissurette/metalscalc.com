Privacy Policy

How SpotCalc handles data: the calculator runs in your browser, stores settings locally, and does not require an account.

Overview

SpotCalc is designed to be private by default. The calculator runs entirely in your browser. Your weights, purities, saved item lists, and calculations are processed on your device and are not transmitted to or stored on our servers.

Local storage on your device

To make the tool convenient, SpotCalc saves a few preferences in your browser's local storage, including your saved item list, selected metal and purity, dark-mode preference, and row display settings. This data stays on your device and can be cleared at any time by using the Clear & Reset button or clearing your browser data.

Third-party spot price feed

To display live prices, the page requests current gold, silver, platinum, and palladium quotes from gold-api.com. These requests are standard web requests and do not include your calculator inputs.

Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand aggregate, anonymous usage (such as page views and general device type) so we can improve the site. Any analytics in use are listed here and do not identify individual users. We do not sell personal data.

Cookies

SpotCalc does not use advertising cookies. Any storage used is functional (the local preferences described above) or, where analytics are enabled, limited to aggregate measurement.

Your choices

You can clear all locally stored SpotCalc data at any time. Because no account is required, there is no personal profile to delete on our side. Questions can be sent via our Contact page.
