Silver Analysis

Silver momentum desk.

Silver blends precious metal psychology with industrial demand and higher volatility.

Desk note

Silver can move late, fast, and violently. Confirm momentum before assuming it is simply cheap.

Current Read

Signals to watch

MomentumImproving

Silver strength becomes more reliable when it outperforms gold for multiple sessions.

DemandCyclical

Industrial usage can help, but recessions can pressure the metal even when gold is firm.

VolatilityHigh

Position size matters because silver routinely overshoots both up and down.

Framework

Silver analysis checklist

RatioGold/silver ratio

A falling ratio means silver is outperforming gold; a rising ratio means caution.

DemandIndustrial cycle

Solar, electronics, and manufacturing demand shape the long-term story.

PremiumsRetail coin and bar spreads

Silver premiums can be large enough to change the true breakeven price.

RiskBreakout quality

Look for strong closes and follow-through instead of one-day spikes.

Use Case

Before buying or selling silver

Process

Price silver with room for premiums.

Silver can be attractive when the ratio is high, but retail premiums and shipping can erase the advantage if the metal has to rise a lot before breakeven.

  • Compare spot value against actual dealer premiums.
  • Check the gold/silver ratio for relative value.
  • Expect wider swings than gold.
