Process

Price silver with room for premiums.

Silver can be attractive when the ratio is high, but retail premiums and shipping can erase the advantage if the metal has to rise a lot before breakeven.

  • Compare spot value against actual dealer premiums.
  • Check the gold/silver ratio for relative value.
  • Expect wider swings than gold.
FAQ

Silver market analysis questions

Why is silver more volatile than gold?

Silver has a smaller market and a large industrial demand component, so it swings more than gold in both directions. It often outperforms in strong rallies and falls harder in selloffs.

How does industrial demand affect silver prices?

About half of silver demand is industrial — solar panels, electronics, and electrical contacts. Strong manufacturing and solar growth support prices, while industrial slowdowns can weigh on them.

What does the gold-to-silver ratio tell me?

It shows how many ounces of silver equal one ounce of gold. A high ratio suggests silver is cheap relative to gold; a low ratio suggests the opposite. Many investors watch it for relative value.

Is silver a good hedge against inflation?

Silver can hedge inflation like gold, but its industrial side makes it more cyclical. It may lag gold when growth is weak and lead when growth and inflation rise together.
